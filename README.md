## 1. Project Overview

## 2. Strategic Insights
* **Service Excellence:** "Cabin Staff Service" consistently ranks as the highest-performing metric...
* **The "Value" Gap:** Despite high staff ratings, "Value for Money" sits at a lower average...
* **Fleet Performance:** The **Boeing 787 and A380** show significantly higher "Seat Comfort" scores...
* **Geographic Sentiment:** North American and European routes show the highest volume of feedback...

## 3. Technical Implementation
* **Data Transformation:** Cleaned and structured unstructured review data...
* **Calculated Fields:** Developed custom formulas in Tableau to segment sentiment...
* **User Experience (UX):** Integrated global filters and "Reset" functionality...

## 4. Recommendations
